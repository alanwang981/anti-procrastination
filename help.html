Anti-Procrastination Help Guide

Getting Started

  1. Add websites to your Whitelist (productive sites) and Blacklist (distracting sites)
  2. Set focus duration in minutes and enable Focus Mode
  3. View reports to track your productivity over time

Whitelist

These are sites you want to use during focus sessions (e.g., work-related sites)

Format Examples:

  • example.com - Matches exactly example.com
  • *.example.com - Matches all subdomains like docs.example.com

Blacklist

These are sites you want to block during focus sessions (e.g., social media)

Behavior:

  • Automatically redirects you if you try to visit
  • Shows a warning page with options to close or go back

Focus Mode

  • Blocks access to all blacklisted sites
  • Shows warnings when visiting non-whitelisted sites
  • Tracks time spent on different categories of sites
  • Shows countdown timer for your focus session
  • Notifies you when session ends

Productivity Reports

  • View time spent on whitelisted vs blacklisted sites
  • See your productivity ratio (percentage of time on good sites)
  • Compare daily/weekly/monthly performance
  • All time tracking is precise to the second

Tips

  • Use wildcards (*.domain.com) to cover all subdomains
  • Start with shorter focus sessions (25-50 minutes)
  • Regularly review your reports to identify distractions
  • Update your lists as your work habits change
